Toad in the Hole

Here is your recipe for Toad in the Hole

This reciple is simple, nutrituous and only takes 20 minutes to make!

Follow the instructions below to make some delicious Fabada:

Ingredients

  • 125 g self-raising flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • pinch turmeric
  • 300 ml unsweetened soya milk
  • Â½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 100 ml water
  • 8 vegan sausages

Step-by-step instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flours, baking powder, salt and turmeric.
  2. In a jug, measure the soya milk, mustard and water and mix well.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a frying pan and cook the sausages gently until their skins are brown all over. Remove from the heat.
  4. Carefully remove the hot oil from the oven, pour the batter into the base of the tin then place the sausages and onion wedges in the batter.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes on a low oven shelf, until the batter is puffed and golden. Serve with gravy and green vegetables.
